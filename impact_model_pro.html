1. Time Efficiency (Human Capital)

Most individuals struggle with the complexity of "Old vs New" tax regimes. Researching deductions, 80C, 80D, and HRA rules takes hours of manual effort.

  • Assumed Hourly Value: ₹500/hr (Avg. salaried professional)

  • Monetary Value of Time: ₹2,750 per user.

2. Financial Gain (Tax Optimization)

A significant percentage of taxpayers overpay because they fail to calculate the "breakeven point" for deductions. Our Tax Wizard handles this edge case automatically.

  • Estimated Overpayment: ₹10,000 to ₹50,000 annually per user.
  • System Impact: Direct recovery of these funds through mathematical identification of the optimal tax regime.
  • Scaling Value: For every 1,000 users, the system recaptures ₹2.5 Crore in unnecessary tax leakage.

3. Long-term Readiness (FIRE Goal)

By providing a Money Health Score and a FIRE Path Planner, the system shifts user behavior from passive saving to systematic investing.

  • Behavioral Shift: Users who see a clear "target number" are proven to increase their SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) contributions by 20-30%.
  • Quantification: Reducing the "Retirement Gap" for the Indian demographic by providing elite-level corpus planning to the masses.
